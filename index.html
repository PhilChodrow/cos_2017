Course Description

Welcome to the course repository for 15.S60, Computing in Optimization and Statistics. This is an advanced course offered by and for practicing researchers in fields relating to operations research, computer science, applied mathematics, and computational engineering. Here you'll find complete materials for each session, as well as descriptions of the pre-work necessary prior to each. You can also find the most recent course syllabus.
